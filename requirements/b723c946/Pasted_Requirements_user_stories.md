# User Stories: E-Commerce Platform

**Total User Stories:** 6
**Estimated Effort:** 16 days

## Sprint Week 1: User Authentication

### US1: User Registration
Support user registration through email and password authentication as well as optional Google OAuth integration, with mandatory email verification.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- FR1

**Linked FRs:** FR1

## Sprint Week 2: User Authentication

### US2: User Login
Provide login functionality using JWT-based authentication and may include OAuth integration, with account locking after five failed login attempts to enhance security.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- FR2

**Linked FRs:** FR2

## Sprint Week 3: Product Management

### US3: Product Browsing
Enable users to browse products by category, brand, price range, and ratings.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- FR3

**Linked FRs:** FR3

### US4: Product Search
Provide keyword-based search with auto-suggestions and filtering options such as price, rating, and availability.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- FR4

**Linked FRs:** FR4

## Sprint Week 4: Checkout and Payment

### US5: Shopping Cart
Include shopping cart and wishlist functionality, allowing users to add or remove products, update quantities, and view real-time price calculations. Wishlist features will enable users to save products for later and move them to the cart when desired.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- FR5

**Linked FRs:** FR5

### US6: Checkout Process
Allow users to select delivery addresses, choose shipping options, select payment methods, and confirm orders. The payment integration will support credit and debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets, and it will properly handle payment success and failure scenarios.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- FR6

**Linked FRs:** FR6
